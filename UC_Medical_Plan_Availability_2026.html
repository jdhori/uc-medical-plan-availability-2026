UC Medical Plan Availability in 2026

Where you live and/or work can limit the medical plans available to you.

PPO Plans

These PPO plans are available without a ZIP or county lookup:

  • UC Medicare Choice Throughout California PPO through UnitedHealthcare. Non-Medicare family members may use UC Blue & Gold HMO through Health Net.
  • HealthSavings+, UC Care, UC High Option, UC Medicare PPO (±Rx) Throughout the USA PPO plans through Anthem Blue Cross.
  • HealthSavings+, UC Care Worldwide PPO plans through Anthem Blue Cross.

Notes for UC HMOs

  • Both ZIP code and matching (overlapping) county must be entered for an accurate result.
  • If a plan does not appear above, that plan is not available.
  • Availability may differ between Medicare and non-Medicare HMOs through the same insurance company.
  • If a county does not appear in the dropdown list, HMOs are not available in that county.
  • To be eligible, employees must live or work in the service area of an HMO.
  • To be eligible, retirees must live in the service area of an HMO.

Coverage Outside California

Eligible retirees who have a home address outside of California, but remain in the USA (not U.S. territories), get separate, non-UC coverage through Via Benefits if they:

  • Are notified by UC/Via Benefits that they are eligible for the program,
  • Are age 65+ and covering only age 65+ dependents,
  • Are enrolled in Medicare Parts A & B, with all covered dependents enrolled in Parts A & B.

Plan availability through Via is dependent upon the state/county of residence; contact Via directly to determine plan availability.
